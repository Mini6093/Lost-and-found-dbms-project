How to report your lost item at RVU

If you've lost an item on the RVU campus, follow these steps:

  1. Check the "View Lost Items" page to see if your item is listed.
  2. If not, go to "Report Lost Item" and submit the form with item details.
  3. Your report will be added to the system for others to view.
  4. If a match is found, the RVU Lost and Found office will contact you.
  5. For help, contact the RVU Lost and Found office directly.
